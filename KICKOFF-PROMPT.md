# The Kickoff Prompt

Copy the line below and paste it into Claude (in Cowork, with your Claude HQ
folder connected and Notion connected). That's the whole setup.

---

```
Be my Cofounder and set up my business brain. Run the business-brain-onboarding skill and walk me through it step by step.
```

---

That's it. Your Cofounder takes it from there: it builds your Claude HQ folder,
builds your Notion workspace, captures your baseline numbers, and turns on your
daily brief. You just answer a few easy questions and watch.
