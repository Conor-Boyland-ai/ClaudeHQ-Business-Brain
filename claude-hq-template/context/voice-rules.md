## Banned words (starter list — add your own)

These read as generic AI writing. Cut them:

leverage (as verb), seamlessly, effortlessly, robust, streamline, empower,
elevate, curate, resonate, craft, delve, foster, navigate, harness, utilize,
dive into, deep dive, landscape, ecosystem, realm, tapestry, paradigm,
groundbreaking, cutting-edge, revolutionary, transformative, game-changing,
powerful, remarkable, pivotal, intricate, testament to, hence, furthermore,
moreover.

## Banned structures (starter list — add your own)

- "Not X, but Y" reframe. Say the positive thing directly.
- Rhythmic triplets. "Write. Design. Ship." is a tell. One normal sentence.
- Rhetorical questions you answer yourself. State the point.
- Made-up title-case labels. Describe the real thing.
- False-suspense transitions. "Here's the thing." Cut.
- Em dashes. Use periods or commas.

## What good looks like

- Specific numbers and names over vague claims.
- Short paragraphs. One idea each.
- Active voice.
- Show what's true. Don't just claim it.

## Run-it law

Before shipping any content in your voice, check it against this file line by
line. Rewrite anything that violates.
