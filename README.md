# Business Brain

Your AI business brain, installed in one step.

This plugin gives you a Cofounder agent and sets up your entire business brain:
a **Claude HQ** folder on your computer and a matching **Claude HQ** workspace in
Notion. Your Cofounder builds both for you, captures where your business stands
today, and starts briefing you every morning so your AI is always up to date and
never forgets.

## What you need first

- Claude desktop app with Cowork
- A Notion account, connected to Claude
- A folder on your computer called `Claude HQ`, with Claude's file access pointed
  at it

## Setup (about 15 minutes, you mostly watch)

1. Install this plugin.
2. Open Cowork with your Claude HQ folder connected and Notion connected.
3. Paste the kickoff prompt (see `KICKOFF-PROMPT.md`):

   > Be my Cofounder and set up my business brain. Run the
   > business-brain-onboarding skill and walk me through it step by step.

4. Answer the easy questions as your Cofounder asks them. Watch it build.

When it's done you'll have a working business brain in both places, your baseline
numbers captured, and a daily brief running.

## What's inside

- **Cofounder agent** — knows your business, holds your state, briefs you daily.
- **Business Brain Onboarding skill** — the guided setup your Cofounder follows.
- **Claude HQ template** — the starter folder structure, filled in for you during
  onboarding.

## What comes next
